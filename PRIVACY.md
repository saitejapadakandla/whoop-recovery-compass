# Privacy Policy for Recovery Compass

Recovery Compass is a personal local tool for accessing my own WHOOP data.

The app may request access to WHOOP profile, body measurement, recovery, cycle, sleep, and workout data. The data is used only for personal recovery, sleep, strain, workout analysis, habit tagging, and local coaching summaries.

Data is not sold, shared, transferred to third parties, or uploaded to a hosted service by this app. OAuth tokens and fetched data are stored locally on the user's machine.

Access can be revoked at any time through WHOOP account settings.
